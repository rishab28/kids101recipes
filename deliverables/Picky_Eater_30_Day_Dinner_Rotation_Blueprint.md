# Picky-Eater 30-Day Dinner Rotation Blueprint
*30 Days of Kid-Approved, Nutrient-Dense Indian Dinner Plans That Eliminate Dinner Struggles*

---

## Introduction: Why Dinners Are Hard
At the end of the day, children are tired, sensory sensitivity is high, and parental patience is low. This blueprint provides a 30-day rotation system that balances comfort food textures with dense pediatric nutrition.

### The Golden Strategy:
1.  **Shared Ingredients:** Dinners utilize prep that can easily feed parents too (see the Family Health Blueprint for the parent's conversion).
2.  **Texture Blending:** Pureeing vegetables into sauces (creamy paneer makhani, dal tadkas) avoids visual triggers.
3.  **Low-Stress Presentation:** Small portion sizes with a "comfort component" on the plate.

## Section 1: The 4-Week Rotation Matrix

| Week | Theme | Core Carb | Hidden Nutrient Source |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Week 1 | Soft Comfort Foods | Rice & Soft Grains | Dal lentils & pureed gourds |
| Week 2 | Interactive Dinners | Stuffed Roti & Rollups | Paneer, tofu, & minced spinach |
| Week 3 | Visual Favorites | Colorful Puris & Chillas | Beetroot, pumpkin, & sweet potato |
| Week 4 | Western-Indian Swaps | Pasta & Healthy Burgers | Fortified sauce purees & legume patties |

---

## Section 2: Week 1 & 2 Dinner Plans

### Week 1: Soft Comfort Grains
*   **Day 1:** Creamy Moong Dal & Lauki Khichdi, dolloped with cow's ghee. Served with curd.
*   **Day 2:** Steamed Idlis with Hidden Veggie Sambar (sambar vegetables pureed post-cooking).
*   **Day 3:** Soft Rice with Creamy Yellow Dal Tadka + soft boiled carrot circles on the side.
*   **Day 4:** Veggie-fortified Rava Upma (cooked soft with finely grated carrots and green peas).
*   **Day 5:** Dahi Rice (curd rice) with a tempering of mustard seeds, curry leaves, and grated cucumber.
*   **Day 6:** Soft Ragi Ganji cooked with milk, cardamom, and a touch of date paste.
*   **Day 7:** Paneer & Green Peas Mash served with soft wheat paratha.

---

### Week 2: Interactive Rollups & Wraps
*   **Day 8:** Spinach Paratha Roll (spinach blanched and pureed into the dough) stuffed with soft paneer crumble.
*   **Day 9:** Cheese & Vegetable Whole Wheat Quesadilla (minced capsicum, sweetcorn, and cheese melted inside).
*   **Day 10:** Beetroot Hummus Wrap (pink hummus spread on soft wheat roti with cucumber strips).
*   **Day 11:** Soft Tofu & Carrot Bhurji rolled inside a warm ghee roti.
*   **Day 12:** Oats & Suji Cheela stuffed with mild potato-paneer mash.
*   **Day 13:** Dal-fortified Wheat Puri served with dry potato bhaji.
*   **Day 14:** Scrambled Egg Roll (whole wheat wrap lined with a thin egg omelet and rolled).

---

## Section 3: Week 3 & 4 Dinner Plans

### Week 3: High-Color Sensory Favorites
*   **Day 15:** Pink Beetroot Chilla (grated beetroot blended into besan batter) served with sweet curd.
*   **Day 16:** Golden Pumpkin Soup served with toasted whole wheat garlic bread sticks for dipping.
*   **Day 17:** Orange Sweet Potato Paratha (boiled sweet potato mashed directly into the dough) served with butter.
*   **Day 18:** White Rice with Creamy Kadhi (besan-yogurt curry) topped with soft steamed spinach pakodas.
*   **Day 19:** Mixed Veg Cutlets (boiled potato, beetroot, paneer, and peas pan-seared with oats coating).
*   **Day 20:** Green Moong Dal Dosa (Pesarattu) served with mild coconut chutney.
*   **Day 21:** Carrot-fortified Creamy Sooji Kheer (sweetened with date powder).

---

### Week 4: Healthy Kid-Friendly Swaps
*   **Day 22:** Hidden-Veggie Pasta (durum wheat pasta tossed in a sauce made from pureed tomatoes, carrots, and bottle gourd).
*   **Day 23:** Baked Potato & Paneer Sliders (whole wheat slider buns with a pan-seared high-protein patty).
*   **Day 24:** Whole Wheat Veggie Pizza (thin crust spread with tomato-carrot sauce, topped with mozzarella cheese).
*   **Day 25:** Soft Rice with Creamy Rajma (kidney bean curry cooked until mashable).
*   **Day 26:** Oats & Banana Pancakes cooked in ghee and served with fresh fruit slices.
*   **Day 27:** Paneer Tikka Skewers (mildly spiced paneer cubes baked soft with bell peppers).
*   **Day 28:** Vegetable Hakka Noodles (made with wheat noodles, lots of cabbage, carrots, and scrambled egg/paneer).
*   **Day 29:** Moong Dal Idli with mint-coriander curd dip.
*   **Day 30:** Celebration Dinner: Creamy Paneer Makhani (cashew-tomato base) with soft butter naan/roti.
