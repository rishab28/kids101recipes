# Kids' Gamified Habit Board & Rewards Tracker
*Fun, Interactive Printable Trackers for Healthy Eating Habits*

---

## Introduction: Gamifying Nutrition
Children respond far better to positive reinforcement and play than to rules and instructions. This Habit Board transforms daily meals into a game where they can color stars, track progress, and earn healthy rewards.

### How to Use These Boards:
1.  **Print Them Out:** Print these pages in color and stick them on your refrigerator or bedroom wall at eye-level for your child.
2.  **Use Stickers or Crayons:** Let your child color a star or place a sticker *themselves* immediately after they complete a habit.
3.  **Set Clear, Small Rewards:** Agree on a weekly non-food reward (e.g., a trip to the park, choosing the family movie, or playing a favorite board game). Do not use chocolate/sweets as rewards.

## Board 1: The Veggie Explorer Chart
*Try 7 new veggies in 7 days. Color the matching badge when you take a bite!*

-   **[ ] Day 1: The Green Tree (Broccoli / Spinach)**
    *   *Task:* Take 1 bite of broccoli or spinach.
    *   *Color: Green* 🥦
-   **[ ] Day 2: The Red Power (Tomato / Beetroot)**
    *   *Task:* Taste a dish with tomatoes or beetroot purees.
    *   *Color: Red* 🍅
-   **[ ] Day 3: The Orange Eyes (Carrots / Pumpkin)**
    *   *Task:* Eat 2 carrot circles or a spoon of pumpkin mash.
    *   *Color: Orange* 🥕
-   **[ ] Day 4: The White Crunch (Cauliflower / Radish)**
    *   *Task:* Taste steamed cauliflower or grated radish.
    *   *Color: Light Yellow* 🥬
-   **[ ] Day 5: The Purple Wonder (Brinjal / Purple Cabbage)**
    *   *Task:* Take a tiny bite of cooked brinjal or purple cabbage.
    *   *Color: Purple* 🍆
-   **[ ] Day 6: The Golden Sweet (Sweetcorn / Yellow Pepper)**
    *   *Task:* Try a sweetcorn kernel or yellow bell pepper strip.
    *   *Color: Gold* 🌽
-   **[ ] Day 7: Explorer's Choice!**
    *   *Task:* Try any vegetable prepared in a new way.
    *   *Color: Your favorite color!* 🎨

## Board 3: The Star Rewards Table
