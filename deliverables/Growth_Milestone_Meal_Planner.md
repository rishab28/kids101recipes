# Growth Milestone Meal Planner
*Age-Specific Nutrition Targets & Daily Meal Plans for Kids Aged 2 to 12*

---

## Introduction: Dynamic Nutritional Needs
Children are not miniature adults. Their metabolic rates are faster, bone growth requires high calcium, and brain development requires essential fatty acids. A 3-year-old toddler has completely different portion and macro needs than a 10-year-old school-goer.

This meal planner breaks down nutritional requirements by age milestones, offering realistic daily meal templates.

---

## Section 1: The Three Growth Milestones

### Milestone 1: The Toddler Years (Age 2–4)
*   **Focus:** Brain development (fat-heavy) and establishing food acceptance.
*   **Daily Targets:**
    *   *Calories:* ~1,000 kcal
    *   *Protein:* 13g to 15g
    *   *Calcium:* 700mg (for teeth and early bone framework)
*   **Meal Strategy:** Small, frequent meals (5-6 times a day). Keep textures soft. Avoid force-feeding; focus on food exposure.

---

### Milestone 2: The Primary Schooler (Age 5–8)
*   **Focus:** Sustained energy for school, play, and immune defense.
*   **Daily Targets:**
    *   *Calories:* ~1,400 kcal
    *   *Protein:* 19g to 22g
    *   *Iron:* 10mg (to prevent anemia and support cognitive focus)
*   **Meal Strategy:** Incorporate high-protein tiffins that stay fresh for 4 hours. Keep dinners light but fiber-rich for deep sleep.

---

### Milestone 3: The Pre-Teen (Age 9–12)
*   **Focus:** Skeletal growth spurts and early hormonal stabilization.
*   **Daily Targets:**
    *   *Calories:* ~1,800 to 2,000 kcal
    *   *Protein:* 30g to 35g
    *   *Calcium:* 1,200mg (peak bone density building window)
*   **Meal Strategy:** Higher portion sizes. Ensure healthy fats (ghee, nuts, seeds) are abundant to support hormone synthesis.

## Section 2: Macro & Portion Matrix
Use this table to estimate daily portion sizes based on your child's age group:

| Food Group | Portion for Age 2-4 | Portion for Age 5-8 | Portion for Age 9-12 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Grains (Roti/Rice)** | 1 roti or 1/2 cup cooked rice | 1.5 rotis or 3/4 cup rice | 2-3 rotis or 1.5 cups rice |
| **Protein (Paneer/Dals/Eggs)**| 30g paneer or 1/2 cup dal | 50g paneer or 3/4 cup dal | 100g paneer or 1.5 cups dal |
| **Milk / Dairy (Curd)** | 1.5 cups (350ml) | 2 cups (500ml) | 2 cups (500ml) |
| **Fats (Ghee/Nut Butter)** | 1.5 tsp daily | 2 tsp daily | 1 tbsp daily |
| **Veggies & Fruits** | 1 cup total | 1.5 cups total | 2.5 cups total |

## Section 3: Daily Milestone Menu Templates

### Sample Day Plan: Age 2-4 (Soft & Frequent)
*   **Breakfast (8:00 AM):** 1 soft Oats-Banana Crepe (Snack Vault recipe) with milk.
*   **Mid-Morning (11:00 AM):** 3-4 Yogurt Drops (frozen) or small banana slice.
*   **Lunch (1:30 PM):** Soft Moong Dal Khichdi mashed with ghee and 2 tbsp curd.
*   **Afternoon (4:30 PM):** 1/2 apple sliced thin with a smear of almond butter.
*   **Dinner (7:30 PM):** Steamed Idli soaked in mild veggie-pureed sambar.

---

### Sample Day Plan: Age 5-8 (Energy & Focus)
*   **Breakfast (7:00 AM):** Oats & Veggie Savory Muffin + 1 small glass turmeric milk.
*   **Tiffin / School (10:30 AM):** Spinach Paratha Roll stuffed with grated paneer.
*   **Lunch (1.30 PM):** Yellow Dal with brown rice, green beans salad, and roasted papad.
*   **Afternoon (4:30 PM):** A handful of crispy makhana + sliced fresh orange.
*   **Dinner (7:30 PM):** Hidden-Veggie Pasta (tomato-carrot sauce base) with tofu chunks.

---

### Sample Day Plan: Age 9-12 (Growth Spurt Power)
*   **Breakfast (7:00 AM):** 2 paneer besan chillas + 5 soaked almonds.
*   **Tiffin / School (10:30 AM):** 2 boiled eggs (sliced, seasoned) OR High-Protein Chickpea Salad.
*   **Lunch (1.30 PM):** Rajma curry with white rice, cucumber-tomato salad, and curd.
*   **Afternoon (4:30 PM):** Chocolate Date & Nut Energy Bite + 1 glass fresh coconut water.
*   **Dinner (7:30 PM):** 2 whole wheat rotis kneaded with sattu flour, served with Paneer Makhani and sautéed broccoli.
