## Introduction: The Kid Protein Dilemma
Protein is critical for cell growth, muscle repair, and immune response in growing children. However, many kids reject high-protein foods like meat, fish, boiled eggs, or chunks of paneer due to texture aversion.

This master system offers 40+ methods to secretly fortify your child's favorite everyday dishes with clean, natural proteins without changing the flavor or texture.

## Section 1: The 10 Protein Fortification Boosters
Keep these high-protein powders and purees prepped in your kitchen. Add them to routine foods:

1.  **Sattu Powder (Roasted Chickpea Flour):** 20g protein/100g. Mix into roti dough.
2.  **Moong Dal Powder:** Dry roast yellow moong dal and grind to a fine powder. Add to soups/curries.
3.  **Whey Protein (Unflavored):** 80% concentration. Blend into smoothies and pancake batters.
4.  **Nut & Seed Powder:** Dry roast almonds, walnuts, pumpkin seeds, and chia seeds. Grind fine. Add to milk.
5.  **Paneer & Tofu Puree:** Blend paneer/tofu with water into a cream. Use as curry base or spread.
6.  **Sattu Sattu Butter:** Mix sattu powder with ghee and honey to replace commercial chocolate spreads.
7.  **Egg White Whip:** Whisk egg white into warm milk porridge at the very end of cooking (completely odorless).
8.  **Soy Flour:** Blend 20% soy flour into regular wheat flour.
9.  **Chickpea Puree:** Blend boiled chickpeas and fold into regular mashed potato or cutlet mixtures.
10. **Lentil Water (Stock):** Cook dals with double the water. Strain and use the high-protein stock to knead dough.

## Section 2: 10 Stealth Protein Breakfast & Snack Recipes

### Recipe 1: Secret Protein Pancakes
*Fluffy sweet pancakes with 12g protein per serving.*
*   **Ingredients:** Rolled oats (1/2 cup), Sattu powder (2 tbsp), Ripe banana (1), Milk (1/2 cup), Egg (1) or curd (2 tbsp).
*   **Stealth Hack:** Sattu and oats provide complex carbs and plant protein, completely masked by banana.

---

### Recipe 2: Paneer-Stealth Paratha
*Soft paneer grated so fine it melts into the dough.*
*   **Ingredients:** Whole wheat flour (1 cup), Paneer (50g, grated with microplane), Ghee (1 tsp), Warm water.
*   **Stealth Hack:** Grating paneer on a microplane grater turns it into a powder that integrates into dough without texture chunks.

---

### Recipe 3: Oats & Moong Dal Cheela
*Savory crepes with 15g protein per serving.*
*   **Ingredients:** Yellow moong dal (soaked, 1/2 cup), Rolled oats (1/4 cup), Ginger (1/2 inch), Salt.
*   **Stealth Hack:** Blend everything with water into a smooth batter. Cook like a dosa. The oats cut down the strong lentil flavor.

---

### Recipe 4: Creamy Nut-Butter Apple Sandwiches
*Replacing sugary jam with natural seed butter.*
*   **Ingredients:** Apple slices, homemade pumpkin seed + almond butter (grind roasted seeds to butter).
*   **Stealth Hack:** Seeds contain up to 30g protein per 100g. Pumpkin seeds are virtually tasteless when ground with almond.

---

### Recipe 5: Soy-Fortified Veggie Cutlets
*Pan-seared potato patties with soy granules.*
*   **Ingredients:** Boiled potatoes (2), Soy granules (1/2 cup, soaked and minced fine), Breadcrumbs, Salt.
*   **Stealth Hack:** Soy granules mimic the texture of minced potato and absorb any seasoning.
