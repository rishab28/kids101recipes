The Strategy

The "Swap, Shape, Sneak" Framework

Our recipes aren't complex diets. They run on a simple 3-step formula:

🔄

1. SWAP (Smarter Ingredients)

We swap refined flour (maida) with fiber-rich oats/ragi, and white sugar with dates or jaggery. The taste stays delicious, but the nutrition increases 5x.

⭐️

2. SHAPE (Creative Presentation)

Kids are drawn to fun visuals. We shape boring parathas into pinwheel rolls and idlis into mini smileys. They feel like they're eating fun market foods!

🕵️‍♀️

3. SNEAK (Hidden Nutrition)

We hide spinach in green flatbread dough and beetroots in pink pasta sauce. Your child eats their favorite foods while getting full nutrition without a fuss.
